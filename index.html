Citely

Welcome to Citely! Citely is a citation manager that allows you to track your research as you perform it. Make sure you attribute your sources properly, by taking the hassle out of bibliography management.

Citely is GPL, meaning you can run this code at your site, and not need to expose your data to a third party if you don't want to. Citely stores citations for scholarly research on the network, allowing users to tag citations in a folksonomy. Organize and export citations to common bibliographic formats. Allow the data and access to be managed by you!
